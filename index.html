NA LCS PLAYOFF PICTURE

by Derek 'Kathix' Adams and Benson 'Sarcasmappreciated' Li

The regular season of the 2015 NA LCS spring split concludes next week with every team playing two games. With 10 games left in the season, there are a total of 1024 (2^10) different potential outcomes in the final standings. We generated all 1024 outcomes and analyzed how each result would affect the final standings.

The first table below shows the probability of every team finishing in places 1 thru 10 (including ties). Hovering over a team name will display a second table that has the breakdown of the raw tie scenarios for the potential finishes. The results assume that each team has a 50% chance to win each game, and the values in the tables below represent percentages.
